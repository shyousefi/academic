About Me

I am a dedicated researcher in the field of Natural Language Processing (NLP) with a focus on Large Language Models and their application to low-resource languages. My research aims to address bias and fairness issues in AI systems, particularly for underrepresented languages and communities.

Currently, I am collaborating with the University of Konstanz on a data-driven survey on bias and fairness in low-resource languages. My work combines technical expertise with a deep understanding of linguistic and cultural contexts to create more equitable NLP technologies.

I am fluent in English and Persian, native in Kurdish, and have elementary knowledge of German. My interdisciplinary approach combines computational methods with linguistic insights to develop innovative solutions for language processing challenges.
